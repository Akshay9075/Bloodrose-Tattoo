About Blood Rose Tattoo

Creating unique, custom designs that tell your story

Our Story

Blood Rose Tattoo was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: to create exceptional tattoo art in a safe, welcoming environment. What started as a small studio has grown into one of the most respected tattoo shops in the region, known for our commitment to quality, safety, and artistic excellence.

Our founder, Sarah Black, envisioned a space where clients could feel comfortable expressing their creativity while receiving the highest standard of professional tattoo services. Over the years, we've assembled a team of talented artists who share this vision and bring their unique perspectives to every piece they create.

We believe that every tattoo tells a story, and we're honored to be part of that storytelling process. Whether it's a small symbol with deep personal meaning or a large custom piece, we approach each project with the same level of care and attention to detail.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Safety First

We maintain the highest standards of sterilization and safety protocols. Every piece of equipment is properly sterilized, and we follow strict health department guidelines to ensure your safety.

Artistic Excellence

Our artists are constantly honing their skills and staying current with the latest techniques and trends. We believe in pushing artistic boundaries while maintaining timeless quality.

Client Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our priority. We work closely with each client to ensure their vision is realized and they're completely happy with their tattoo experience.

Community

We're proud to be part of our local community and support various local events and charities. We believe in giving back to the community that has supported us.

Our Studio

A professional environment designed for your comfort and safety

Private Rooms

Each artist has their own private workspace, ensuring privacy and comfort during your tattoo session. Our rooms are designed to be relaxing and professional.

Sterilization Station

Our dedicated sterilization area ensures all equipment is properly cleaned and sterilized. We use hospital-grade autoclaves and follow strict protocols.

Consultation Area

A comfortable space for discussing your tattoo ideas, reviewing designs, and planning your perfect piece. We take the time to understand your vision.
