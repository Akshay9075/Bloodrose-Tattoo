Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or visiting our studio during business hours. We recommend booking a consultation first to discuss your ideas.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring any reference images, ideas, or inspiration for your tattoo. We also recommend bringing a photo ID and being prepared to discuss your budget and timeline.

How much do tattoos cost?

Our pricing varies based on size, complexity, and artist experience. We provide detailed quotes during consultations. Prices typically start at ₹150 for small custom pieces.

Do you do walk-ins?

We primarily work by appointment to ensure quality service and proper consultation time. However, we may accommodate walk-ins based on availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you when possible.

How do I care for my new tattoo?

We provide detailed aftercare instructions after your tattoo session. This includes cleaning, moisturizing, and protecting your tattoo during the healing process.
